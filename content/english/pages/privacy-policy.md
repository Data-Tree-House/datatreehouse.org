#### Information We Collect
We use Umami Analytics to understand how visitors interact with our website. Umami collects basic, anonymized data such as page views, referral sources, browser type, device type, and country. No personal information is collected, and no cookies are used.

#### How We Use Your Information
The anonymized analytics data we collect is used solely to understand and improve our website's performance and user experience. We do not use this data for advertising or sell it to any third party.

#### Cookies
We do not use cookies. Umami Analytics is a privacy-friendly, cookieless analytics tool, so your visit to our site will never be tracked across other websites.

#### Third-Party Services
We use Umami Analytics as our sole analytics provider. Umami is designed with privacy in mind and does not collect personally identifiable information. You can learn more at [umami.is](https://umami.is).

#### Privacy Policy Changes
1. We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
2. Continued use of our website after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
